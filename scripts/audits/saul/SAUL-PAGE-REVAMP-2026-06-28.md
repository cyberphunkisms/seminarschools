# Saul page revamp

## Purpose

The page now begins as a readable professional profile and continues into the complete career record.

## Structure

1. Professional identity and contact details
2. Short account of teaching, curriculum, and public learning
3. Direct routes to Leizu Academy, Seminar Schools, and the current CV download
4. Three current-work cards
5. Career archive with quick views, timeline and practice arrangements, detailed filters, role entries, certifications, and education

## Interaction changes

- Quick views set the existing archive filters for current work, teaching and education, or the full record.
- Timeline and practice arrangements are explicit controls.
- Detailed category filters, sharing, and PDF export live inside the archive tools rather than preceding the professional profile.
- Existing click to expand role entries, print output, theme choice, language choice, text scaling, and Creative Mode behavior remain available.

## Visual changes

- The desktop content area is wider.
- Career dates, role titles, notes, and expanded entries use clearer scale and spacing.
- The geometric watermark is quieter in professional mode and remains available in Creative Mode.
- The page retains the Seminar Schools visual system without placing geometry over core reading areas.
