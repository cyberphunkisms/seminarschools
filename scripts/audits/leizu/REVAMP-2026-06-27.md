# Leizu public site revamp

## Scope

This revision rebuilds the public Leizu experience while leaving the paid booking system intact.

Changed pages

- `/leizu/`
- `/leizu/intake/`
- `/leizu/policies/`
- `/leizu/scholarship/`
- `/leizu/donate/`
- `/leizu/teach/`

Unchanged payment and booking files

- `leizu/payment-config.js`
- `leizu/booking-button.js`
- `leizu/booking-success/index.html`
- every Netlify function in `netlify/functions/`

## Main page changes

- A clear first-screen statement of the service
- Three distinct actions for plans, subjects, and Chinese consultation
- A visible Chinese consultation route in the universal navigation and the contact section
- A shorter sequence that explains the approach, subjects, course catalogue, session format, plans, teacher, access, and intake
- Readable subject, format, pricing, FAQ, and contact layouts on desktop and mobile

## Chinese consultation

The consultation route is preserved and promoted in three locations on the home page and one location across every supporting page.

`https://calendly.com/kyrah0131/leizu`

## Policy protection

No price, payment arrangement, cancellation window, refund term, eligibility threshold, or program promise was changed on the policy page.

## Checks run

`npm run verify:all`

All existing critical, geometry, register, payment, and Leizu pipeline checks pass.
