# Leizu localization and funnel fixes

This release fixes the audit items selected for implementation.

- Every intake CTA carries current selected courses, selected ESL support, the active Forest, IB, or OSSD path, and a saved language preference.
- Forest Year payment schedules have French, Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, and Persian dynamic copy.
- Simple English tracks automatic ESL flags separately from manual requests. Turning it off removes its own flags. Clear removes all course-level flags.
- The Leizu home page saves and restores language selection. Supporting Leizu pages preserve the language preference and clearly identify their current English content while their full translations are prepared.
- Persian sets the semantic document direction to RTL.
- All courses use a consistent ESL-support rule.
- Chinese terminology corrections use 研討課, 研讨课, 核心单元, and 默认学生已具备.
