# Upcoming GTA Humanities Lectures — May 8, 2026 forward

Compiled May 8, 2026. Quantity over curation. Toronto-area open public lectures in philosophy, humanities, social sciences. Each entry has a verified source URL.

## Caveat

This snapshot hits the academic dead window. Spring term ended late April. Summer term is research-only at most departments. Fall 2026 calendars rarely post before late August. This roster is shorter than the April 15 corpus. The April list anchored on departmental colloquium series running through spring; those series are now between cycles.

Major loss to flag at the top. The 2026 Congress of the Humanities and Social Sciences, which the April 15 search would have rolled into June, is virtual-only this year. The Federation announced in 2025 that the in-person edition is cancelled, with a smaller-scale in-person event at a yet-to-be-determined location. The 2025 in-person edition at George Brown was the last under the old format. Source: https://universityaffairs.ca/opinion/cancelling-congress-wounds-humanities-research/

The CSHPS PhilEvents listing dated May 30 - June 6, 2026 at George Brown is a stale rollover from the 2025 entry and should be ignored. Source: https://philevents.org/event/show/130042

**May 8, Friday, 9:00am - 6:00pm. Conversations Across Borders: Black Theatre in the US and Canada.** Jackman Humanities Institute. Room 100, Jackman Humanities Building, 170 St. George Street. One-day symposium launching the Black Theatre Lab. Schedule: First panel "Lineages of Black Performance in Practice" with Djanet Sears, Lisa Karen Cox, Kaja Dunn (9:45-11:15am). Networking lunch (11:30-1:00). Second panel "Documenting Black Performance" with Faedra Chatard Carpenter, Cheryl Thompson, Naila Keleta-Mae (1:30-3:00). Keynote "Black Performance Now: Artistic Directors Reflect" with Mumbi Tindeybwa Otu (Obsidian Theatre) and d'bi.young anitafrika (Watah Theatre) (3:15-4:45). Staged reading by BCurrent directed by Natasha Adiyana Morris (5:00-6:00). Free and open to public. Source: https://www.humanities.utoronto.ca/events/conversations-across-borders-black-theatre-us-and-canada
