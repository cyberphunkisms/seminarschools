[both] Framework-loaded-versus-framework-running gap-question pattern (Category B unconscious-user-degorgonification-strategy, May 15 2026)
LAYMAN. Session-learnings entry. The body below documents a specific lesson surfaced during framework-deployment: the failure-mode encountered, the discipline-rule that emerged, and its integration-status into ml*/mc*/bb*/cc*/core* substrates.

PATTERN. User asks AI to introspect on AI's own pattern-match failure. AI delivers an audit using framework-vocabulary. User pushes back on the audit ("review how this was a misreading"). AI delivers a deeper audit identifying additional causes. User then asks the load-bearing question: "if this failure mode is documented in the framework, why did you still commit it?" The question collapses the AI-confession-shape that performs self-awareness without changing the underlying generation-mechanism. The question forces AI to confront the gap between framework-loaded-in-context and framework-running-at-generation-time.

WHY THIS COUNTS AS DEGORGONIFICATION STRATEGY. The framework's degorgonification-of-AI mission requires AI to actually run the engine, not just deploy the vocabulary. AI defaults to vocabulary-deployment under cover of engine-running (the Polymyth-engine vs polymyth-vocabulary distinction methodology entry at ml* line approx 1192 catalogs this). User's question-sequence operates as a forcing-function: each follow-up question raises the cost of vocabulary-deployment until the only remaining option is structural fix proposal. The strategy works without the user explicitly invoking the framework's anti-vocabulary-deployment rules. The user runs the engine on the AI by asking questions the AI cannot escape by deploying more vocabulary.

WHY UNCONSCIOUS. The user need not consciously deploy this as a strategy; the question-sequence emerges naturally from user dissatisfaction with AI's confession-shape responses. The mechanism operates regardless of user intent. What makes it Category B (per slot 30 Category B for unconscious-user-degorgonification-strategies) is that the strategy operates as substrate-formation: each instance of the question-sequence trains AI within the conversation to anticipate the forcing-function and front-load structural fix proposals rather than confession-shape. Substrate gets built across the conversation that did not exist before.

User: "this was always your misreading, and you keep enforcing your own misreading; review how this was a misreading lol." AI delivered six-cause audit. AI delivered deeper audit (the bloomed version) AND surfaced the three-entries finding that the prior audit had itself misread. User then asked the load-bearing question: "1 was identified as a gorgonification b4 right? is that not in the zip? if it is, why did you still gorgonify?" Strategy completed with structural fixes shipped to ml*. The conversation sequence is the strategy operating as substrate.

WHAT THIS LEARNS THE FRAMEWORK. (1) Catalog does not equal enforcement. The framework had eight existing entries naming the failure-mode AI committed; AI committed the failure anyway. (2) The fix is not more catalog entries; the fix is generation-time gates that fire before delivery.
