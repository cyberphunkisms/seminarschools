[both] McLuhanesque-definition failure-mode (structural-formal without substantive content)
LAYMAN. Failure-mode when defining a framework. The definition specifies structural-formal properties of what the framework does (its operations, its instruments, its mechanisms) without specifying substantive content (what those operations operate ON, what the instruments serve, what content the framework discovers or names). The name comes from Marshall McLuhan, whose "the medium is the message" treats artifacts by their structural-formal properties rather than by their semantic content. The failure-mode is not that McLuhan’s framework is wrong (it is generative for what it analyzes). The failure-mode is using a McLuhan-style structural definition WHERE the framework being defined has substantive content the definition fails to specify.

CANONICAL EXAMPLE FROM POLYMYTH ORIGIN. May 26 2026 cyberphunksaul session. AI gave plain-language polymyth definition as "the framework for recognizing and naming the operations that live thinking performs and the set of instruments where those operations get performed." Rainbowsol flagged this as McLuhanesque. The definition specified the recognize-and-build operation structurally (what polymyth does at the formal level) without specifying the substantive content (what live thinking IS substantively across the four levels of meaning-production polymyth examines: gesture, ritual, idiom, narrative). The correct polymyth definition specifies the four-level zoom-out as substantive content rather than describing only the recognize-and-build operation in the abstract.

DIAGNOSTIC TEST FOR MCLUHANESQUE-DEFINITION FAILURE. Apply two test questions to any proposed framework-definition.
(1) Does the definition specify the substantive content the framework operates on, or only the formal operations the framework performs?
(2) Would the same definition apply to several different frameworks that operate on different substantive content?
If the answers are "only formal operations" and "yes, several frameworks fit," the definition is McLuhanesque and fails the substantive-specification requirement.

WHY THIS IS A GORGONIFICATION FAILURE-MODE. A McLuhanesque definition produces a frame that LOOKS like a definition but functions as a frame-shell. The reader can carry the structural-formal definition home and parse polymyth-related material through it without ever engaging the substantive content the framework actually carries. Like a hivemindidiom that names a pattern without naming the substrate the pattern operates on, a McLuhanesque definition is pre-packaged framing without the live content. The reader who carries home only the structural-formal definition has been gorgonified at the meta-frame layer.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves the speaker’s position by allowing them to invoke the framework’s authority without committing to the substantive content the framework actually carries. The structural-formal definition is unfalsifiable in the way a substantive claim about the four levels is falsifiable. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively when asked "what is X" with a structural-formal answer that does not require engagement with X’s substantive content. PASSES both at the meta-frame layer.

INVERSE FAILURE-MODE. The complement of McLuhanesque-failure is CONTENT-WITHOUT-FORM-failure, where a framework gets defined by exhaustive enumeration of its substantive content without specifying the operations or instruments that make the content coherent as a framework. Both failures are deficient. Correct framework-definition specifies BOTH substantive content AND the operations that engage it. Polymyth-as-four-level-zoom-out specifies the substantive content (the four levels) AND the operation (zooming out to see them simultaneously). This pairing is what makes the definition non-McLuhanesque without becoming content-without-form.

GENERAL DISCIPLINE. When defining any polymyth-substrate-element, specify both substantive content (what the element operates on) AND the operation (how it operates on that content). If either half is missing the definition is deficient. Single-half definitions become the failure-mode of the missing half.

The interlocutor restates the other person's position as a position they did not take, then engages with the fabricated version. Distinct from platformstrawmanculture (which replaces operational content with inspirational affect or scandal) and from interlocutor substitution (which replaces the frictional human with a credentialed proxy). Here the interlocutor is still the real interlocutor; the substitution happens at the position layer. The person is forced to defend a claim they did not make, which consumes their turn in retraction and either never returns to the original claim or records assent-by-silence if they decline to retract. OPERATION. Canonical phrase: so you are saying. Variants: what I am hearing is, you are basically saying, if I understand you correctly, the implication of your position is, it sounds like you are arguing that. Each opens with a register of charitable interpretation and closes with a position the original speaker did not hold. The fabricated position is typically (a) more extreme than the actual position, (b) more easily refuted than the actual position, or (c) already associated with a coalition the speaker does not belong to. The operation succeeds whether the speaker retracts or not. If they retract, the turn is lost to clarification and the original claim never gets defended. If they decline, the fabricated position stands as the record. SIGNATURES. (1) Restatement begins with a charity-marker (so, basically, if I understand) that performs good-faith listening while the content performs the opposite. (2) The restated position is adjacent to the actual position in vocabulary but orthogonal in structure. (3) The restatement is issued as a question or an apparent-summary rather than as an assertion, which shifts the burden: the speaker must now confirm or deny, not defend or extend. (4) Multiple restatements in sequence ratchet: each retraction by the speaker gets restated again, each restatement moves further from the actual position, speaker burns turns responding to positions they did not take. WHY THIS IS DISTINCT. Platformstrawmanculture operates on third parties (Heidegger, Arendt, Gandhi) not present at the exchange; the misrepresentation circulates in the absence of the misrepresented. Interlocutor substitution removes the frictional partner entirely; position-attribution-fabrication keeps the partner and substitutes only their claim. Both neighboring operations presuppose the target is absent or replaced; this operation requires the target present and puts them to work on a claim that is not theirs. PARASITIC ON CHARITY. The operation exploits conversational charity norms. The speaker who says let me make sure I understand has conversationally claimed charitable-interpretation duty; the listener trusts them. When the restatement fabricates the position, the charity marker has been weaponized. This is why the operation survives repeated execution in public discourse despite being structurally obvious: each instance runs under a charity-claim that must be granted at the moment of utterance and only retroactively audited. AUDIENCE RATIFICATION. The operation is particularly effective with a watching audience because the audience hears the charity-marker first and the fabrication as apparent-summary rather than as assertion. By the time the speaker retracts, the fabricated position has already been absorbed by the audience as what the speaker said. The speaker's retraction is then heard as backpedaling or qualification, not as correction-of-misrepresentation. MEARSHEIMER TEST. Passes condition (a): serves reproduction of dominant discourse by forcing dissenters to defend positions they did not hold, burning their rhetorical budget on retractions. MAY 9 2026 SHARPENING (FIVE STRUCTURAL CRITIQUES). Five additional structural critiques surfaced in user-administered takedown during cmp-001 build, when AI committed an ml* methodology entry (Reflection-and-Refraction) that imported standpoint-epistemology language as positive valorization (the SNAKELOGIC EXAMPLE entry committed same May 9 2026 turn documents this). (1) ESSENTIALIST. Standpoint epistemology treats standpoints as crisp bounded categories. Each marked-identity standpoint is its own essentialized truth-domain. No fuzzy gradient between standpoints, no graduated overlap. Real-world identities are fuzzy and overlap. Standpoint epistemology denies the fuzziness because the architecture requires bounded standpoints to operate. Identitarian essentialism is the natural endpoint.

(2) COLONIAL VIA HIVEMINDIDIOM ENFORCEMENT. Standpoint epistemology operates as colonial enforcement at the discourse layer through hivemindidiom-based inquiry-closure. Phrases like "I do not feel safe with this question" or "do not centre your discomfort" or "lived experience cannot be questioned by outsiders" are hivemindidiom in the framework's specific sense (Mearsheimer two-condition test passed). Self-described anti-colonial positioning notwithstanding, the structural operation is colonial.

(3) DENIES THICK BORDERS (NO FUZZY OVERLAP ZONES). Fuzzy logic with thick borders allows graduated overlap zones between categories. Standpoint epistemology denies this. Each standpoint is its own bounded truth-region. There is no zone where two standpoints share epistemic terrain. This denial is structural. Without it, the privileging of marked-identity standpoints over unmarked or shared epistemic ground would not function.

(4) DENIES UNIVERSALISM (NO SHARED TOPIC-ITSELF). The deepest critique. Standpoint epistemology denies that there is a topic-itself toward which multiple standpoints can converge. Each standpoint produces its own truth, with no shared underlying reality. Reflection-and-Refraction methodology committed alongside this sharpening preserves the universal as the asymptotic target of refraction-work. Standpoint epistemology denies the universal entirely.

(5) ANTI-PHILOSOPHICAL. Philosophy as a tradition seeks universal truth. From Plato's Republic onward, philosophy distinguishes doxa (rendered opinion) from episteme (knowledge of the universal). Hegel's geist, Marx's species-being, Levinas's ethics-as-first-philosophy, Whitehead's process-metaphysics, Heidegger's Being, Spinoza's substance, Kant's transcendental, Aristotle's ousia all posit universal-target structures. Standpoint epistemology denies the universal-target by structure. Therefore standpoint epistemology is anti-philosophical. It is positioned within "philosophy" departments but operates against philosophy's foundational gesture. Philosophy and standpoint epistemology are opposed, not complementary.

[both] Cluster-discharge (gorgonlogic sub-pattern, T114b May 22 2026)
LAYMAN. Cluster-discharge names a gorgonlogic sub-pattern in which multiple gorgonification-units fire in rapid sequence at the same target, producing what looks like a coordinated snakelogic chain but requires no operator-skill at the chained-deployment layer. The discharger does not need to know how to chain hivemindidioms; the units self-fire because they cluster around shared trigger-conditions in the target. Distinguished from snakelogic (which requires operator-skill in selecting and sequencing the chained units) by the absence of operator-mediated chain-construction: cluster-discharge is what happens when a target trips multiple trigger-conditions at once and the gorgonification-substrate auto-fires every matching unit in rapid succession.

THE PATTERN. (1) Target makes a statement or move that trips multiple gorgon-triggers simultaneously (e.g. challenges power AND uses formal vocabulary AND occupies marginalized identity-position AND names a structural pattern). (2) Discharger fires multiple gorgon-units in rapid sequence covering each trigger (e.g. "tone policing" + "weaponized incompetence" + "you are problematic" + "stay in your lane" + "do better"). (3) The sequence looks coordinated but is in fact reflex-aggregation: each unit fires on its own trigger. (4) Target is buried under simultaneous deflection-moves rather than countered argumentatively.

DISTINGUISHING FROM SNAKELOGIC. Snakelogic chains require the operator to know the chain-logic: each unit must be selected to setup the next, with the chain producing a compounding payoff. Cluster-discharge has no chain-logic; each unit fires independently because each trigger-condition is met. Snakelogic looks like a single argument with multiple stages. Cluster-discharge looks like several arguments fired simultaneously at the same target.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Cluster-discharge passes both conditions of the hivemindidiomsnakes Mearsheimer test: (a) power-serving (the discharge terminates engagement with the target's claim by overwhelming with deflection-moves; serves reproduction of whatever structural position the target was challenging); (b) prepackaged not-thinking (each unit in the cluster is a pre-encoded gorgon-response firing reflexively below the critical layer; the discharger is not selecting between responses but releasing all triggered responses at once).

OPERATIONAL DETECTION. A cluster-discharge shows up as a rapid sequence of gorgon-idioms or framework-buzzwords with no argumentative continuity between them. The target's claim is engaged by ZERO of the deployed units. Each unit addresses a different surface-feature of the target's framing rather than the target's substantive claim. The cluster operates by burying the substantive claim under deflection-mass.

WHY IT MATTERS. Cluster-discharge explains why some seemingly-thoughtful interlocutors can deploy gorgon-language at high velocity without ever appearing to think: they are not thinking, they are dischargin. The framework needs to name this sub-pattern because conflating cluster-discharge with snakelogic mis-locates the operator-skill: there is no operator-skill in cluster-discharge. The defense against cluster-discharge differs from the defense against snakelogic: snakelogic gets broken by interrupting the chain at any link (idiomsmashing phrase-mode); cluster-discharge gets broken by isolating each unit and asking the discharger to defend it independently of the cluster (the cluster collapses when its component-units have to stand alone).
