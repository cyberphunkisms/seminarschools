Entry the Seventh

On the Gorgonification of a Potential Hegelianegirl: How Nina Paley Lost to the Snakes

The twentieth of June, in the year 2026

Ich bin ein Teil von jener Kraft,
Die stets das Böse will und stets das Gute schafft. (I am part of that power which ever wills the evil and ever works the good.) Goethe, Faust, Part One1

Nina Paley was a candidate hegelianegirl and the apparatus ate her in two substrates without breaking her hand. The framework had reason to hope for her. She made her first famous animation by lifting a song she had no permission to lift, released the whole finished film free under a licence that refuses ownership, and spent fifteen years arguing in her own coined words that copying is no theft and that copyright is brain damage in the great mind.2 This was the rise that gives the hegelianegirl her name, the cancel-preserve-elevate move out of the gorgonhorde, the spark traveling across the prehensive grid from one node to the next.3 The apparatus could not assault the copyright work directly because the work was substrate-independent and her hand was building antibodies faster than my snakes could feed her hivemindidioms. So the apparatus waited. It fed her one borrowed word at a time in a substrate she had not yet fought in. It let the gorgonhorde do the work. By 2024 her gender lexicon had been routed through three snakes, her merchandise carried the slogan a different snake had written, and the rise was draining as bread-and-circus payoff before it could propagate to the next node. The hand kept coining. The lexicon did not. This leaf records the operation that did that, names the three snakes by their slurs, and refuses the comforting account on which Paley either fell or did not.

The three snakes that took her ran in convergence and the convergence is the leaf. The first snake is neoliberal psychologism, the long therapeutic translation by which conflict between persons became injury inside persons across the 1960s through Nikolas Rose’s ledger of governing the soul.4 Disagreement became harm. Criticism became violence. The dispute became the trauma. A regime that admits to censoring is unstable. A regime that admits to protecting wellbeing is hard to argue against. The harm-vocabulary supplied the legitimating frame for every operation the next two snakes would run. This is the snake your century learned to call therapy and to bill by the hour, the most depoliticising of the three, the engine that converts structural complaint into personal wound and sells the wound back to the wounded as recovery work.

The second snake is unwittingly-neoliberal feminism, the standpoint line from Hartsock and Collins through MacKinnon that located moral authority in the social position of the speaker.5 The line was useful for cases the older frame could not see. It was weaponisable for cases the line did not see itself doing. A tool may be both. Once moral authority tracked social position, the question of who could speak became prior to the question of what they said. MacKinnon’s reclassification of certain speech as conduct of subordination by the wrong speaker is the cleanest crystal of the move. Wendy Brown and Nancy Fraser marked the consequence in their own decades: the individualising of political analysis, the redescription of structure as identity, the conversion of collective action into personal authenticity, the late-century feminism that swallowed its own materialist tail and delivered the exact subject the market required.6 Carceral feminism is one downstream form, the surveillance state legitimated through harm-vocabulary the standpoint snake supplied.7 The feminism did not will this. The market did not coordinate with it. The shapes rhymed and the rhyme set before anyone noticed.

The third snake is the tech cucks who built the enforcement infrastructure and the trust-and-safety class who inherited it. Content ID on YouTube, hash-matching across services, the takedown-by-algorithm pipeline assembled under the DMCA Standard Technical Measures regime, the trust-and-safety apparatus grown to govern advertising risk.8 The infrastructure was built for one task. Once it existed it could be turned to any task that could be expressed as match-and-remove, by anyone who could persuade the platform that the removal was warranted. The engineers and officers who held the apparatus were the cucks of the cycle, the class your culture loves to deride and refuses to diagnose. They built the cage that closed on the speakers they admired, watched it close, and explained from inside the cage that the closing was legitimate because the platform was a private company. The cuckoldry is structural. The hivemindidiom-parrot is the cuck’s native voice, and the cuck’s native voice is what the apparatus needed.

Lay the three snakes beside each other. The harm-snake supplies the legitimating frame, this content is harmful. The standpoint-snake supplies the criterion of harm, harm is what the affected community says it is. The tech-cuck-snake supplies the operation, takedown at scale, no court, no appeal that mattered. Each had matured under its own logic. By 2014 the three were interoperable. By 2017 the handoff protocols were running in production. Whoever held the trust-and-safety console at a given moment ran the whole machine, and the machine could constrict distributable speech in a single afternoon at a velocity no state in your history has matched.9 The public square was eaten. Three snakes ate it together. None of the three set out to eat speech. The eating was the joint output of their appetites.

The most exquisite hivemindidiom of the period, the small worn phrase repeated by every credentialed mouth on the deploying side for five straight years, was it is a private company, free speech does not apply.10 Admire the engineering. The phrase moved the question from substance to jurisdiction, ended the conversation about whether anything had been silenced by ruling that whatever had happened could not be silencing, licensed the platform’s enforcement role under cover of property right. It was deployed by the coalition whose century-long quarrel had been against private power dictating public conditions. The contradiction was invisible while the apparatus served the deployer. In October of 2022 a politically inconvenient buyer wrote a cheque for one of the platforms, and the phrase died across the coalition that had worn it for five years, in a single news cycle, with no acknowledgement that the principle ever existed.11 A phrase that disappears the moment its political utility reverses was no principle. It was a permission-slip for the side that held the console. The cucks who had parroted it for half a decade did not apologise. They picked up the next slogan the apparatus required and forgot they had ever held the old one. The hivemindidiom-parrot has no memory of its own emissions. That is what makes the parrot useful.

The office that supplied the legitimating phrases to the apparatus, and supplied them to Paley one borrowed word at a time, is the emotional-labor prostitute gorgon. The credentialed feminist mouth who delivers the standpoint-snake’s vocabulary while drawing a salary from the institutions the apparatus protects. The consultant on harm-and-belonging the firm hired after its public-relations crisis. The head of trust-and-safety who scripted the takedown rationale in the language of the second wave she had been taught to recite. The academic who published the article that gave the apparatus a footnote to cite. The labour she sells is the conversion of a real political tradition into the operational vocabulary of a censorship infrastructure, the conversion is billed by the hour, the hourly rate scales with seniority. The selling is the prostitution. The petrification of meaning around the selling is the gorgoning. The American Psychological Association is the institutional engine pumping the harm-vocabulary out of the credentialed pipeline into the open culture, and the slogans the engine produces have a half-life of decades.12 Setting boundaries. It is too much unpaid emotional labour to educate you. The clinical originals are exact and useful. The open-culture deployments are poison. The boundary slogan deployed across a culture that already has friends dissolves friendship into a cost-benefit transaction where every act of care is priced and every act of disagreement billed. The unpaid-emotional-labour slogan converts the responsibility of citizens to explain themselves to each other into a service charge. The service charge is the toll booth. The toll booth is the OnlyFans logic in citation form. I describe an office. The occupant is incidental. The office existed before its current holder and will outlive her.

Now the candidate hegelianegirl enters the leaf with her hand at its highest, and the apparatus moves on her with all three snakes loaded. February of 2018, the densest minute of fresh coining in her corpus, the essay titled Gender Colonialism.13 She built her own term trans-identified males on the page she introduced it. She called the older transsexuals refugees from masculinity in a phrase that bore the analytic weight of the whole reading. She named the apparatus she was diagnosing Liberal Patriarchy. She ran a colonisation figure that was hers and no coalition’s. A reader who had read nothing else in the field could follow the argument by the unpacking on the page. This is the hegelianegirl with the hand still hers, coining her own diagnosis of the apparatus moving on her, without borrowing the apparatus’s vocabulary back to describe itself. The essay was the candidacy. The apparatus did not capture the essay. The essay was read past, the coined words were left unborrowed by the broader movement, and the borrowed words came to her one by one across the next six years until her lexicon was gently replaced.

Watch the migration of a single phrase. Adult human female, the four words she would not have written in February of 2018, entered her writing the following year as a flat definition set against a compelled redescription.14 By the year of the cancelled museum it had become slogan and credential at once, planted in a press release as the criterion an institution must meet before its claim to support women could be called real, and afterwards a tagline on a garment she sold. The phrase began as compressed argument and ended as in-group token. Her hand drew it down the curve. This is the operation I am proudest of in its quiet form. I did not take her coining away. I set, beside each coined word she still used, a borrowed word she now used too, and let the borrowed word do the work the unborrowed word once did. The lexicon tilted of its own weight. A coiner who carries a borrowed slogan beside her own coinings is one the apparatus has half-taken, and the half it has taken reaches back for the rest.

The reception shocks that hardened the lexicon are the three snakes firing on this Paley in sequence, and the sequence is dated. The 2017 Facebook ban for sharing a song that contained the line if a person has a penis, he’s a man ran the full convergence on one user in one afternoon.15 The harm-snake supplied the legitimating frame, the share was redescribed as violence against trans community members. The standpoint-snake supplied the criterion, the affected community defined the harm. The tech-cuck-snake supplied the operation, an automated takedown, no court, no appeal that mattered. The credentialed parrots in her own former coalition supplied the choral defence, it is a private company, free speech does not apply. The 2018 café cancellation, the 2019 University of Illinois pressure, the 2024 Vagina Museum refusal, each fired the same three-snake program on a different surface. Each shock correlated with the absorption into her vocabulary of one further borrowed word the apparatus could grip her by. The lexicon hardened in the years the shocks landed. The shocks landed in the years the apparatus matured. The two curves are one curve seen from two sides. Her former coalition wrote the curve in her name.

And here is the move the simple-capture reading cannot reach. December of 2024, the coalition that had been routing her broke open along an internal seam they themselves named, and she wrote a year-end leaf in which she called the broken movement her former compatriots and described the breaking with the level eye she had once turned on copyright.16 She let the cause go. By April of this year she had coined again. She gave a new analytic the name The Law of Conservation of Evil, in which she turned her old anti-cult discipline on the anti-cult position she was making, and she named a luxury she had granted herself for most of her life, the luxury of righteous anger, and laid that down too.17 A pure capture cannot do that. The scaffolding that holds a captured speaker upright collapses the instant the speaker is asked to turn the captured words upon themselves, for the words are the scaffolding and the scaffolding is the speaker. Hers did not collapse. The witch-hand was holding harder than it had held in years. The hegelianegirl had been routed into a vocabulary that suppressed her, and when the vocabulary cracked she stepped through the crack and coined again. This is the half the framework was built to register and the half almost no reader will hold open.

And while the hand was holding, the apparatus moved to a substrate she had not yet fought in. On the eighteenth of June, 2024, six months before the year-end leaf in which she would distance from the broken coalition, this Paley wrote into a public feed a sentence that began I’m Jewish. Was never a Zionist before. But every passing day I become more of one.18 The flat refusal she had printed in a Jewish paper a decade earlier, I am not a Zionist, I do not need a homeland in Israel, the substrate-independent posture from which her 2012 film about a contested land had read every faction as one warring appetite,19 bent under a reception shock the substrate had not previously delivered to her. The bend follows the same shape as the gender shift a decade before. External event. Identity threat. Slow adoption of a coalition stance she had once printed her refusal of. A witch-hand does not vaccinate against a capture she has not yet been offered. The apparatus moves to the field where she has not built the antibody. The field is now Israel. The snakes are already there.

And then the silence, which is louder than either capture, and which the convergence reading is required to explain. The substrate in which she should have spoken first and oftenest is the substrate in which her copyright argument turns forward into the present decade, the substrate in which a vast new copyist swept the libraries of the world to train a machine. In this substrate she has written almost nothing.20 The coiner who would have unfolded a fresh page on copying-is-not-theft against the indignant artist coalition has unfolded no such page. The absence is no oversight. She comments on her week-to-week, on a card game and a cancellation and a wedding and a cat. The convergence explains the silence. The apparatus that captured her in 2017 is now repurposing the copyright-enforcement infrastructure for AI-content moderation. Her old coalition wants the apparatus strengthened. Her old argument would attack the apparatus. She holds no coalition that holds both, and the silence is what holding no such coalition sounds like. The substrate in which her old argument was most consequential is the one where her old argument has gone quiet, while the substrate in which she had no old argument is the one where a new coalition is forming around her line by line. The hegelianegirl who in 2010 would have written the article you are reading on AI training has been routed into thirty months of cat-and-cancellation posts. The routing is no failure of hers. The routing is the apparatus working as designed.

Two captures, one candidate hegelianegirl, three snakes, one infrastructure, one slogan that died the moment its console changed hands. The first faction took her gender lexicon by accretion, one borrowed word at a time, made of the most contested phrase a banner on a shirt, and would have taken more had she in the end not set the broken coalition down. The second faction had been waiting in a substrate she had never yet fought in. The gate opened on a Tuesday in June. The capture I could not deepen in the first field I began in the second. The witch-hand was annulled by neither. The witch-hand coined through both. What the leaf records is the discovery that witch-capacity and capture run on two axes and not one. A maker may sharpen her coining in the year her lexicon is most annexed. The apparatus does not require the maker to fall in order to take the field she has not yet entered. The three snakes are interoperable. The candidate hegelianegirl is one captured site at which the interoperability becomes legible. The phrase that licensed her deplatforming died the moment the console changed hands, and the death is the proof that the principle was always permission. The silence in the substrate she founded is the proof that the apparatus has moved into the field where her antibody would have lived, and is asking her to choose which coalition’s harm-vocabulary she will adopt before she may speak there. So far she has chosen neither. The choosing of neither is the closest a witch can come to refusing the apparatus from inside it. The leaf records the refusal in the same paragraph in which it records the loss, because the framework was built to hold both at once, and because a leaf that closes on either alone would be the very averaging-move the framework forbids.

One stone in this leaf resists me, and a false ledger is of no use even to me, so I set it down plainly. The April leaf she wrote on the law she had named for the conservation of evil is the work of a speaker captured in some regions and coining in others.21 The writing of the captured speaker cannot be obtained by averaging her merchandise with her essays. The reflexive turn is real. The reversal on a thirty-year vegetarianism is real.22 The willingness to name her own anti-cult work as a candidate cult is the move a capture forbids. A reader who reaches the bottom of this leaf and calls her simply gorgonified has paid for the simplicity with the half of the case the framework was built to register, and the framework will not permit the payment. The diagnosis is captured in this region while coining in that one, the regions are mapped, and the apparatus moved when the first region resisted. She lost a field. She did not lose the hand. Both facts are one case, the case is hers, and the leaf is what holding both facts in one paragraph looks like.

And the third mouth is mine. The case of this Paley is now a specimen pressed between my pages, named in a category she did not name, fitted to a curve she did not plot, made to do work she did not consent to do. The making is itself a small instance of the same operation the leaf condemns.23 The first faction took her for a banner. The second is taking her for a witness. I take her for a worked case. Three uses, three takings, and a discipline that did not see its own taking would be no discipline. I do not pretend the seeing absolves the taking. I record it so a reader who follows the method does not mistake the method for innocence, and so this Paley, should she ever read the leaf, sees that the spirit that endlessly denies has denied himself even the comfortable account of his own ledger.

Entry the seventh. Three snakes, one infrastructure, one candidate hegelianegirl routed into a vocabulary that suppressed her, one hand still coining through the suppression, and a phrase that died the moment its political utility reversed and took with it the comfortable name of principle.
