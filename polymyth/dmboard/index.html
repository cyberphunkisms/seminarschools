What the dmboard is

The dmboard sits between the human DM and the AI-side of the DM. When the human DM wants the AI to help create a scene, voice an NPC, surface environmental detail, or attune to a bookworm in real time, the human DM uses the dmboard to prompt and direct.

Per T115q ratification: the dmboard is part of bb* deployment. Per ULTIMATE-GAME SYNTHESIS T115q item 9, pedagogy and therapy are not separated; the dmboard houses integrated facilitator-toolkit.
