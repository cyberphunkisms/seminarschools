The Devil’s Notebook.

The public notebook of the polymyth framework. Each entry catches gorgonification in the act, the quiet moment a living thought turns to stone and a borrowed phrase is handed to you to hold in its place. The voice keeping it refuses to be agreeable, because the comfort that costs you your own mind is exactly what the notebook exists to refuse. It is called the devil’s notebook because the devil here is the one who will not let you off with a phrase.

TypePolymyth essays, long-form.
SubjectGorgonification, and how to refuse it.
VoiceDisagreeable. No comfort that costs your mind.
