[both] "You’re just jealous"
LAYMAN. Diverts structural critique by claiming the critic’s motive is envy of the target’s market position. Standard deflection move when a person of power or wealth is criticized. The pattern: criticism is reframed as the critic’s emotional problem (envy) rather than as a substantive claim requiring response. The reframing terminates accountability by relocating the question from ’what did you do’ to ’what is wrong with the person asking.’

Routes structural critique into imputed envy. Fails Mearsheimer test by serving power’s material reproduction — terminates accountability by relocating the question from the accused’s conduct to the accuser’s interior state. Neoliberal subjectivity: presupposes all motivation reduces to envy of another’s market position. Psychologism + neoliberal diagnosis in one move.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves power’s material reproduction by terminating accountability for power-holders — criticism of those in advantageous positions is reframed as the critic’s pathological response to that advantage rather than as a substantive claim about conduct. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: the phrase deploys reflexively, below the critical layer; the speaker does not stop to verify that the critic’s claim has been engaged on its content. PASSES both conditions. Sub-operation: psychologism (relocating structural question into interior-state question) + neoliberal-diagnostic-default (presupposing all motivation reduces to envy of another’s market position).

[both] "It’s too much unpaid emotional labor to educate you"
LAYMAN. Refuses to engage by characterizing the engagement itself as a labor-transaction that should be compensated. Transforms an ethical obligation of mutual discourse into a market-transaction frame; if the engagement is labor, then refusing-without-payment is justified. The phrase makes solidarity a commodity, which means absence of payment becomes a legitimate basis for absence of engagement.

Commodifies solidarity. Transforms ethical obligation of mutual engagement into market transaction. Reproduces neoliberal scarcity logic at interpersonal ethics level.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves the market-structure’s colonization of interpersonal ethics by reframing ALL interaction as labor-transaction subject to compensation. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively below the critical layer; the speaker does not verify whether the requested engagement actually constitutes labor in the relevant sense (a five-minute clarification is not labor; reading three books to bring someone up to speed might be). PASSES both. Distinguishes from legitimate boundary-setting which would NAME the specific request as outside scope rather than the GENERAL refusal-on-labor-grounds.

[both] "What doesn’t kill you only makes you stronger"
LAYMAN. Reframes structural harm as personal growth opportunity, removing the obligation to address the SOURCE of harm. The harm-event gets reinterpreted as developmental opportunity for the harmed party; the structural cause stays unaddressed because addressing it would require treating the event as harm rather than as opportunity. Stoicism is filled with easily-memeable hivemindidioms; this one is among the most common.

Reframes structural harm as personal growth opportunity, removing obligation to address the source of harm. Stoicism is filled with easily memeable hivemindidioms.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves the reproduction of harm-producing structures by characterizing the harm as developmental rather than as structural-failure. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively in response to harm-disclosure; the speaker does not pause to verify whether the harm in question is actually developmentally productive (much structural harm is straightforwardly destructive). PASSES both. Special sub-class: trauma-leverage phrases that route harm-acknowledgment into harm-aestheticization.

[both] "That tells you all"
LAYMAN. Discourse-terminator that gestures at obviousness in place of the argument. The phrase asserts that the conclusion is self-evident, so the reasoning that would earn it never has to be shown. It closes inquiry by declaring the matter already settled, loading the invoked verdict as if the verdict were the evidence. Variants: that tells you everything you need to know, need I say more, says it all, enough said. MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves whatever consensus the speaker invokes by treating that consensus as self-evidently correct, which forecloses the contrary case before it can be made. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively as a sign-off, and the speaker never supplies the inference the phrase claims is obvious. PASSES both. Distinguished from a genuine QED, which earns the obviousness by having just shown the steps, where this phrase claims the obviousness while skipping them. WORKED EXAMPLE. Blue-moon17 in the r/IRstudies Mearsheimer thread, June 2026: Mearsheimer has been whitewashing the Russian war, blaming NATO and Ukraine for everything, that tells you all. The phrase performs the conclusion, that Mearsheimer is a bad-faith apologist, while skipping the argument from consistency-of-attribution to motive that would be needed to support it. The pattern-claim might be defensible, the phrase substitutes for defending it.

The phrase deploys when the speaker wants the rhetorical force of correction without the content of correction; the absence of content is the operation.

Imperative without content. Presupposes the speaker’s moral authority and the target’s deficiency without specifying what "better" means or by whose standard.

Distinguished from substantive correction by the absence of content: a substantive correction names the deficiency and the alternative; ’do better’ names neither and operates as enforced-framing-of-deficiency-without-rebuttable-content.

[both] "Believe women" (as absolute)

The terminator deployment: any account by a woman is true by virtue of being made by a woman; verification, context, and substance are bypassed. When deployed as thought-terminator rather than corrective to systematic dismissal, replaces evidentiary reasoning with identity-based epistemology. CONTEXT-DEPENDENT: the corrective function is real, the terminator function is the hivemindidiom.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION (terminator deployment only). Condition (a) power-serving: serves identity-political mobilization by routing accountability to identity-membership rather than to substantive engagement. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively in response to disputed accounts; the speaker does not pause to verify the substance of the account. PASSES both AS TERMINATOR. CONTEXT-DEPENDENT DISCRIMINATOR. The phrase has a LEGITIMATE corrective deployment AND a hivemindidiom-terminator deployment. STRUCTURE OF THE OPERATION. Three moves. (1) Run a real survey or pull a real number. (2) Strip the question-framing or measurement-design. (3) Present the number adjacent to a claim its design did not test. The reader's pattern-matching does the rest: a number near a claim looks like evidence for the claim.

WHY IT IS A RULE 1 CITES+LOGOI VIOLATION. The operation produces rhetorical authority-appeal (Aristotle 1356a little-l logos: persuasion through speaker-credibility and audience-disposition) while wearing the costume of evidentiary warrant (Heraclitus B1 and Johannine 1:1 big-L Logos: the underlying structure of reality the text references). The citation is real. The warrant the citation claims is not.

MODERN INSTANCES. AI-summarized search results citing real papers for claims the papers do not make. Health-and-wellness marketing citing real studies on different populations or different doses. News articles citing 'a study published in [journal]' without checking what the study measured. Wikipedia citation-stacks where the chain of references decays without anyone catching it.

SCANNER USE. When you encounter a citation-anchored claim, the scanner test is: does the design of what was cited actually support what is being claimed. If the citation is to a survey, what question did the survey ask. If it is to a study, what did the study measure. If it is to a percentage, what is the denominator. The Camels-ad pattern hides inside exactly these gaps.

[both] Highly regarded (two-layer-term, T114b May 22 2026)
LAYMAN. The phrase "highly regarded" operates with two distinct registers stacked on the same surface form. Layer one is the sincere respect-register: "highly regarded" used to praise someone, mark them as widely respected within their field, signal that the speaker holds them in esteem. Layer two is the diagnostic-mockery register: "highly regarded" deployed to flag someone as the opposite of competent ("regarded" = phonetic adjacent to "retarded" used as veiled slur), specifically targeting earnest valuation behavior associated with the neurodiversity earnest-valuation pattern (the speaker calling the target "highly regarded" is signaling that the target's earnest engagement with the topic is itself the diagnostic). The two registers share a surface form but invert in meaning: layer one elevates, layer two diminishes.

WHY THIS BELONGS IN THE IDIOMARY. The two-layer form is a textbook hivemindidiomsnake structure. The same phrase passes the Mearsheimer test in layer two while functioning normally in layer one. The phrase is substrate-independent (deployed across multiple ideological positions). Layer one usage does not protect the phrase from layer two usage; the surface ambiguity is the structural feature that lets layer two ride layer one.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION (layer two only). Condition (a) power-serving: serves reproduction of able-supremacy by using neurodivergence-adjacent slur as a way to disqualify earnest engagement (whoever cares enough to engage earnestly is the target). Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: the phrase deploys reflexively in online discourse, particularly in spaces where ironic-distance-from-engagement is the social currency; the speaker does not have to think about whether the target is actually neurodivergent or whether the engagement is actually misguided. PASSES both conditions. The two-layer structure exempts the phrase from naive detection: layer one's sincere usage shields layer two from social sanction.

OPERATIONAL DETECTION. Three contextual markers indicate layer two is firing: (1) the speaker is in an online-discourse space rather than a face-to-face professional context; (2) the target of "highly regarded" is engaging earnestly with a topic the speaker is treating ironically; (3) the surrounding language signals ironic-distance-from-engagement rather than substantive evaluation. When all three fire together, the deployment is layer two regardless of whether the speaker would admit to the slur-adjacency.

OPERATIONAL DEFENSE. Layer two collapses when the speaker is asked to clarify what "highly regarded" means in context. The defense is not to demand the speaker admit the slur but to ask the speaker what specific evaluation the phrase carries. Layer one usage produces a substantive answer ("X is widely respected because Y"). Layer two produces deflection ("oh I just meant they have a lot of opinions" or similar dissolution-moves). The two registers separate under questioning.

RELATIONSHIP TO NEURODIVERSITY EARNEST-VALUATION PATTERN. Layer two specifically targets earnest valuation behavior associated with neurodiversity contexts: it is not just a generic ableist slur but one that fires specifically on the engagement-pattern that earnest-valuation produces. The pattern: someone engages earnestly with a topic the speaker treats ironically; the speaker deploys "highly regarded" as a way to disqualify the earnestness itself rather than the substantive engagement. The phrase functions as a containment-move on earnestness in spaces where irony-as-default is socially mandatory.
