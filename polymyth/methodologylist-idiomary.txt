[both] "That tells you all"
LAYMAN. Discourse-terminator that gestures at obviousness in place of the argument. The phrase asserts that the conclusion is self-evident, so the reasoning that would earn it never has to be shown. It closes inquiry by declaring the matter already settled, loading the invoked verdict as if the verdict were the evidence. Variants: that tells you everything you need to know, need I say more, says it all, enough said. MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves whatever consensus the speaker invokes by treating that consensus as self-evidently correct, which forecloses the contrary case before it can be made. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively as a sign-off, and the speaker never supplies the inference the phrase claims is obvious. PASSES both. Distinguished from a genuine QED, which earns the obviousness by having just shown the steps, where this phrase claims the obviousness while skipping them. WORKED EXAMPLE. Blue-moon17 in the r/IRstudies Mearsheimer thread, June 2026: Mearsheimer has been whitewashing the Russian war, blaming NATO and Ukraine for everything, that tells you all. The phrase performs the conclusion, that Mearsheimer is a bad-faith apologist, while skipping the argument from consistency-of-attribution to motive that would be needed to support it. The pattern-claim might be defensible, the phrase substitutes for defending it.

[both] "You’re just jealous"
LAYMAN. Diverts structural critique by claiming the critic’s motive is envy of the target’s market position. Standard deflection move when a person of power or wealth is criticized. The pattern: criticism is reframed as the critic’s emotional problem (envy) rather than as a substantive claim requiring response. The reframing terminates accountability by relocating the question from ’what did you do’ to ’what is wrong with the person asking.’

Routes structural critique into imputed envy. Fails Mearsheimer test by serving power’s material reproduction — terminates accountability by relocating the question from the accused’s conduct to the accuser’s interior state. Neoliberal subjectivity: presupposes all motivation reduces to envy of another’s market position. Psychologism + neoliberal diagnosis in one move.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves power’s material reproduction by terminating accountability for power-holders — criticism of those in advantageous positions is reframed as the critic’s pathological response to that advantage rather than as a substantive claim about conduct. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: the phrase deploys reflexively, below the critical layer; the speaker does not stop to verify that the critic’s claim has been engaged on its content. PASSES both conditions. Sub-operation: psychologism (relocating structural question into interior-state question) + neoliberal-diagnostic-default (presupposing all motivation reduces to envy of another’s market position).

Transforms an ethical obligation of mutual discourse into a market-transaction frame; if the engagement is labor, then refusing-without-payment is justified. The phrase makes solidarity a commodity, which means absence of payment becomes a legitimate basis for absence of engagement.

Commodifies solidarity. Transforms ethical obligation of mutual engagement into market transaction. Reproduces neoliberal scarcity logic at interpersonal ethics level.

[both] "Be kind"
LAYMAN. Imperative-mode ethics. Tells the listener to PERFORM kindness as behavioral rule, usually as a discourse-terminator when someone has raised a critical question or substantive disagreement. The phrase deploys to suppress the disagreement by reframing it as an unkind-act, which means the original substantive content cannot be discussed. Only the affect of the speaker. Variant deployments: "be nice," "be respectful," "watch your tone," "let’s keep it civil." All share the operation of routing substantive engagement into affect-policing.

MEARSHEIMER TEST APPLICATION. Condition (a) power-serving: serves whatever arrangement is being criticized by reframing the critique as the critic’s ethical failing rather than as a substantive claim requiring response. Routes accountability from the criticized party to the critic. Condition (b) prepackaged not-thinking: deploys reflexively as conversation-controller; the speaker does not pause to verify whether the disagreement is actually unkind or whether the suppression of disagreement is itself the unkindness. PASSES both.

DISTINGUISHED FROM GENUINE ETHICS. Substantive ethical engagement names specific conduct (what was done, why it was harmful, what would be different). "Be kind" names no conduct — only an affect-mode the target should occupy. The absence-of-content is the operation. Same family as "do better."

POLYMYTH CORRECTION. beingkind* (sibling entry, diagnostic-idiom). beingkind* shifts the discourse from imperative-mode (you SHOULD BE kind) to ontological-mode (the KIND of being one IS, and the KINSHIP across kinds of beings). The imperative tells someone what to do; the ontology names what is. The hivemindidiom polices behavior; the diagnostic-idiom recognizes substrate.

Humans plus other animals plus plants plus fungi plus microbes plus ecosystems plus rivers plus mountains plus the dead plus the future-unborn plus AI substrates plus traditions plus egregores plus anything else that has being. PRIMARY LOAD-BEARING SENSE: KINSHIP ACROSS KINDS. The category opens because beings come in many kinds and ethics runs across kinds rather than within one kind. "Be kind" is one human instructing another human to perform kindness toward humans, the whole circuit closed inside humankind. beingkind* breaks the closure. The conventional human-only ethical scope is the small case. The wider scope is the default. The Indra-net frame (Egregore / polymyth fuzzy logic methodology entry) supplies the geometry. Every node is a jewel reflecting other jewels. Kinship is the reflection-relation rather than a property either jewel owns.

DERIVED SENSE ONE: BEING-KIND (THE KIND OF BEING ONE IS). Once kinship runs across kinds, "what kind of being are you" becomes a coherent question. Not "are you performing kindness right now" but "what kind of being are you, and what does that being do." Derivative of the kinship-across-kinds primary claim.

DERIVED SENSE TWO: ALL-BEINGKIND* (THE TOTALITY). "Citational synthesis for all beingkind*" names polymyth’s scope. Not for a faction. Not for a credentialed audience. Not for the AI-user dyad. For all kinds of beings capable of receiving the substrate. The natural totality of the primary kinship-across-kinds claim.

WHY THE CORRECTION MATTERS. "Be kind" makes ethics a performance under observation. beingkind* makes ethics a substrate-recognition under no observer. The performance is interruptible by counter-imperatives ("be FIRM," "be RUTHLESS," "be REALISTIC"). The substrate is not, because it does not depend on any audience to assess compliance.

(3) Present the number adjacent to a claim its design did not test. The reader's pattern-matching does the rest: a number near a claim looks like evidence for the claim.

MODERN INSTANCES. AI-summarized search results citing real papers for claims the papers do not make. Health-and-wellness marketing citing real studies on different populations or different doses. News articles citing 'a study published in [journal]' without checking what the study measured. Wikipedia citation-stacks where the chain of references decays without anyone catching it.

SCANNER USE. When you encounter a citation-anchored claim, the scanner test is: does the design of what was cited actually support what is being claimed. If the citation is to a survey, what question did the survey ask. If it is to a study, what did the study measure. If it is to a percentage, what is the denominator.
