    How this calendar works

    An aggregator pulls listings from venues across the city once a day. Each candidate is checked against four conditions before it appears here. The event has a known time and place. Someone has prepared something to offer. The format invites substantive engagement (not a passive screening unless the filmmaker is in attendance for discussion). And the matter on offer is one a thinking person could find a stake in.

    Events that fail the check are dropped. The list is not exhaustive, by design. Venues currently in rotation:

    • Centre for Ethics, Jackman Humanities Institute, Munk School, Massey College, Hart House, Victoria College, ROM Speaks, and other University of Toronto venues.
    • Toronto Public Library Appel Salon, Type Books, Glad Day Bookshop, and the International Festival of Authors.
    • Revue Cinema, The Royal, Paradise Theatre, Hot Docs Ted Rogers Cinema, and TIFF Bell Lightbox (filtered to events with the filmmaker present for discussion).
    • The Power Plant, Art Gallery of Ontario, and the Museum of Contemporary Art (artist talks and curated conversations).
    • PhilEvents Ontario filter for academic philosophy events not surfaced elsewhere.
    • The Agora at Earl Bales Park, hosted by Seminar Schools.
