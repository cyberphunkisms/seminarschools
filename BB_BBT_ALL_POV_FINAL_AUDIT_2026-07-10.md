## Final status

The public BB surface now presents BookwormBurrows as a voluntary first, AI assisted reading and seminar game. The landing page explains the wormcard path, the human and AI Dimensional Master, assignments as quests, narrative consequences, voluntary seminar use, the full rule library, the pedagogical rationale, and the upcoming BBT research branch.

BBT research existed before this pass as an internal bibliography and conceptual shelf. A playable or clinically usable BBT system has not been built, tested, piloted, validated, or released. The new public BBT page explains the complete current synthesis while preserving that boundary.

## Therapist and practitioner point of view

### Improvements

The new `/bb/bbt/` page states that BBT would be a genuine therapy branch rather than ordinary BB with therapeutic vocabulary added. It explains that a qualified human practitioner would hold the therapeutic relationship, purpose, attunement, interpretation, and professional judgment. AI would remain a support system for preparation, continuity, comparison, and narrative possibility.

The page identifies the practice architecture still required:

- practitioner qualifications and scope
- human controlled AI workflow
- separation of private practice records from player facing CC* memory
- departure, role change, return, debriefing, and bleed processing
- validation across neurodiversity, culture, age, language, privacy, trauma, and therapeutic approaches
- training, pilots, outcome measures, adverse event review, and independent evaluation
- evidence labels that match the actual research

### Remaining work

The complete BBT practice architecture remains unbuilt. BBT remains an upcoming research and design branch.

## Evidence and research point of view

### Improvements

The BBT page synthesizes six research lineages:

1. therapeutically applied tabletop roleplaying
2. bibliotherapy
3. psychodrama and drama therapy
4. bleed and dual consciousness
5. transformative, freeform, and diceless roleplay design
6. attuned human facilitation

The page names candidate mechanisms such as agency, social connection, collaborative problem solving, creative expression, identity exploration, perspective taking, rehearsal, routine, and meaningful group participation. It presents these as research guided possibilities for testing.

The evidence ceiling is explicit. The page states that the literature supports serious design work and careful pilots rather than a claim that BBT is clinically proven. It cites the 2024 scoping review of 51 papers, whose evidence base was predominantly exploratory, along with reviews, qualitative work, small pilots, case reports, practitioner literature, and related roleplay research.

The page contains a selected sixteen source bibliography and routes readers to the larger BBT bibliography.

- controlled evaluation where appropriate

- direct study of BB's diceless literary engine

## Contradiction audit

### Resolved

- BBT research exists while the BBT therapeutic system remains unbuilt.
- BB stays a game and seminar form; BBT would add a real therapeutic relationship and practice architecture.
- AI may support BBT while the qualified human remains the practitioner.
- Emotional modifiers can affect gameplay while remaining contextual and revisable.
- Psychology and Jungian interpretation can be used while unsupported psychologizing remains excluded.
- Voluntary play remains primary while compulsory settings can use a secondary narrative adapter.
- Player facing story logic and backstage professional or institutional records can coexist.

### Still open

No new universal BB mechanic requires a philosophical ruling from this pass. The open work concerns implementation, validation, record architecture, campaign construction, legacy propagation, mobile access, and BBT professional design.
