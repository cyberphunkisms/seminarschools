About this listing

Annual deadline projected from the official October 1 to March 31 cycle. Canadian students in grades 4 to 8 submit original fact, fiction, prose, or poetry up to 5,000 words. Free. Winners become published authors and donate book-sale proceeds to a Canadian charity. Confirm the next cycle on the official site.
