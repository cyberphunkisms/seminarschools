About this listing

Local branch deadlines vary, so this placeholder marks the annual Remembrance contest window. Students submit an original essay or poem on Remembrance in English, French, or bilingual format. Junior essays are up to 350 words, intermediate up to 500 words, senior up to 800 words, and poems are up to 32 lines. Confirm the local branch deadline.
