bookwormburrows·enter the burrow

BB is PolymythDND in classroom form: a teacher-led academic TTRPG where the text becomes the world, wormcards become character sheets, assignments become quests, and AI stays behind the Dimensional Master as the memory clerk.

How BB is played

  1. Choose the anchor text. A story, poem, article, speech, myth, play, or historical document becomes the dimension.
  2. Make wormcards. Each player builds a burrowing character, because the class enters through a wormhole into the text-world.
  3. Give the wormcards to the Dimensional Master. The teacher runs the table. AI helps behind the curtain with schedules, locations, NPC memory, continuity, and record keeping.
  4. Open the burrow without breaking the text. The sealed exegesis stays protected. Play happens around, before, after, beside, and through the world the text makes possible.
  5. Turn assignments into quests. A letter, journal, debate, research note, paragraph, map, scene, or reflection gives the character something to gain, repair, unlock, or risk.
  6. Let academic quality drive consequence. There are no dice. Reading care, writing quality, research, revision, and discussion decide what the world can return.
  7. Keep the teacher as the filter. AI may prepare the bureaucracy of the world, but the teacher decides what reaches the players.
  8. Record the adventure as learning. The table leaves with written work, quests completed, scars, discoveries, open problems, and stronger reasons to keep studying.
