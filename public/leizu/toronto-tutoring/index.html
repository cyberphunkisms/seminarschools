What the work covers

Leizu connects subjects that school systems often separate. A student preparing an OSSD English essay, an IB Extended Essay, a history inquiry, a Theory of Knowledge response, or a university application usually needs the same durable capacities: close reading, source evaluation, a defensible argument, precise prose, and the confidence to revise.

OSSD support

Ontario high-school English, Canadian history, social studies, essay writing, exam preparation, research tasks, and credit-course support.

IB Diploma support

Reading, writing, research, argument, and planning for IB English, history, Theory of Knowledge, Extended Essay, and interdisciplinary work.

Writing and applications

Academic essays, research papers, personal statements, university applications, and the revision process that makes a draft clearer and stronger.

Humanities tutoring

English, philosophy, history, media literacy, and social studies taught through questions that join knowledge across subjects.

How sessions work

Each session begins with the student’s actual work. That may be a reading, an assignment, a research question, an exam, a draft, or a problem that has resisted quick solutions. The session makes the task visible, gives it structure, and leaves the student with a next move they can carry independently.

Private tutoring

One student, one course of work, and direct feedback shaped around the immediate task and the habits behind it.

Small seminars

For students ready to learn in company, where a shared text and serious discussion deepen the work beyond private study.

Questions parents and students ask

Do you offer in-person tutoring in Toronto?

Online sessions are the default. Ongoing Toronto students can arrange in-person sessions after the first few meetings when the format suits both sides.

Can Leizu help with both OSSD and IB?

Yes. Leizu supports OSSD-aligned work and IB Diploma study, while teaching the transferable reading, research, writing, and argument skills that both require.

Can a student begin with one urgent assignment?

Yes. The Starter Block begins with the work in front of the student, then turns that task into a clearer plan for the next one.

What happens before payment?

The intake form establishes the student’s course, goals, and immediate needs. Current policies and pricing are available before a payment link is used.
