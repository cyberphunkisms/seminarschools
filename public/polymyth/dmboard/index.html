BookwormBurrows teacher workflow

The priority AI-assisted workflow for opening a burrow with wormcards, with the teacher as human mediator and a paper or document fallback.

Use the teacher and AI as one Dimensional Master. Choose the anchor text, collect the players' wormcards, open the BB rule file, let the AI prepare the translated in-game characters and campaign state, and keep the teacher as the human table voice. CC* stores campaign continuity. Paper or document play remains available.

Start the table

  1. Choose the anchor text or campaign.
  2. Use Session Zero for character creation, then collect the players' completed wormcards.
  3. Open the BB plain-text manual at /polymyth/bookwormburrows.txt.
  4. Use the manual to prepare quests, time windows, places, people, and consequence paths.
  5. Have the AI translate each wormcard into the chosen world, then keep the teacher as the voice and mediator at the table.

Run the priority AI-assisted version

The teacher uploads the BB plain-text manual, anchor campaign, wormcards, and current CC* state into the chosen AI tool. The AI prepares in-game character translations, story prose, technical resolution, continuity, quest windows, people, places, and consequences. The teacher performs, questions, revises, or overrides the output and decides what reaches the players.

The public website provides the manual, the card maker, and the campaign pages. It does not promise a new DM application.

Keep play grounded

  1. The source text stays protected.
  2. Assignments become quests with real motives.
  3. Work quality drives consequence.
  4. Quest windows can open, close, and echo later.
  5. Weak or missed work creates repair paths rather than permanent shame.
