Why this resource is included

Companion to the Roger apology letter. Students write as one of the children from Bradbury's All Summer in a Day, apologizing to Margot for locking her in the closet during the one hour of sunshine on Venus. Same writing quality assessment. Designed for comparative analysis of two different failures of empathy and two different contexts for apology.
