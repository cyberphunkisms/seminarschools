Why this resource is included

Student-facing interactive campaign page for the Langston Hughes short story Thank You Ma'am. Set in 1958 Harlem on September 20-21. Includes period media (music, newsreel, photographs, front pages), historical context, and the full campaign framework. Runs alongside the BookwormBurrows tabletop system.
