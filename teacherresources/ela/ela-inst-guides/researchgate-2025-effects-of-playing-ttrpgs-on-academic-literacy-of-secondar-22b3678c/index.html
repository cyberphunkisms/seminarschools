April 2025 literature review. Three themes: (1) conceptual framework from play theory, developmental psychology, and literacy research; (2) empirical evidence on reading comprehension, vocabulary, writing skills, narrative competence; (3) engagement and motivation through TTRPGs. Draws on Huizinga 1955 (play rules), Vygotsky ZPD, and implementation barriers research. Free PDF.
