Why this resource is included

Three activities: (1) Symbolism graphic organizer where students locate and interpret symbols in the story with pre-reading informational handout defining symbolism. (2) Hughes biography activity tracing formative years and literary influences. (3) Paired poems worksheet with Harlem, Mother to Son, and I Too by Hughes; students annotate each poem then answer short-answer questions. Free blog walkthrough; worksheets available for purchase.
