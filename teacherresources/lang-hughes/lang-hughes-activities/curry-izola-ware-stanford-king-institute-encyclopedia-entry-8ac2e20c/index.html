Why this resource is included

Authoritative scholarly entry. Izola Ware Curry stabbed King at Blumstein’s Department Store (230 West 125th Street) on September 20 1958 with a 7-inch steel letter opener. More than two hours of surgery at Harlem Hospital. The famous just a sneeze away from death assessment was by attending surgeon Dr. Emil A. Naclerio, quoted in Jet magazine. Cites Papers 4:498-499, King statement October 24 1958, NYT September 22 1958. Free.
