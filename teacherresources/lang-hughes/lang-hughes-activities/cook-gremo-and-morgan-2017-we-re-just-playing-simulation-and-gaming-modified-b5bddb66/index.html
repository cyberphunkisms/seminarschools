Why this resource is included

Qualitative study of 36 middle-school ELA students using a Pathfinder-modified TTRPG paired with Connell The Most Dangerous Game. Found textually grounded gaming decisions, meaningful collaboration, narrative learning through character. The closest published peer-reviewed analogue to what bookwormburrows does. Simulation & Gaming 48(2) 199-218. DOI: 10.1177/1046878116684570. Paid journal; abstract free.
