Why this resource is included

In 1955 DJ Tommy Dr. Jive Smalls brought the R&B Revue concept to Apollo manager Bobby Schiffman. By September 1958 the house was three years into this format with as many as a dozen vocal acts on one bill. Specific September 20 1958 booking not findable in free sources; the Smithsonian Frank Schiffman Collection contains the 5x8 cards that would resolve this. Free.
