## Resolved conceptual questions

### Gesture and trace

The question arose from an AI definition that broadened gesture into any interpretable material difference. The working distinction is that gesture is the first enacted meaning circle. Trace is inherited, carried, or left by gesture, ritual, idiom, and narrative. Trace operates across the levels.

### Ritual and habit

The thesis treats ritual through degrees of salience rather than a rigid necessary-and-sufficient boundary. Habit foregrounds repetition. Ritual foregrounds repetition together with the social, symbolic, inherited, relational, and collective world it carries.

### Ritual and idiom order

The canonical archive order is gesture, ritual, idiom, narrative. Ritual compresses gestures into a repeatable social form. Idiom makes a ritualized script portable in language. Narrative organizes the lower levels into a temporal world. The reverse direction describes activation rather than compression. The ritual-versus-idiom priority remains open for exploration.

### Relation terms

Identity means literal sameness. Equivalence means being treated as equal for a specified operation. Analogy means a shared explanatory pattern. Correspondence means a mapping across systems. Polymythological recurrence means a related movement reappears across distinct myths, rituals, histories, or characters.
